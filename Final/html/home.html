About the Coding Club

Did you ever want to develop industry-level projects? Well, this is the place for you. The coding club offers multiple learning opportunities for a diversity of areas, whether it’s hands-on sessions or online there is no moment put to waste. This also presents you with the perfect time to meet like-minded people with the same passion for technology as you do.

Not only do you learn new techniques and meet new people, but you also get a chance to show your skills in hackathons that will take place throughout the year. This is the perfect time to display your skillset and collaborate with people to deliver a project worth taking first place.

Our goal as the coding club is to prepare young developers for their journey through the industry and build a community that will support each other and work together to solve real-world problems. You also get the added advantage of building a portfolio while improving your skills.
